    Sound Therapy

    Sound therapy is a healing practice that uses different aspects of sound to improve emotional, psychological, and physical well-being. It involves the use of music, voice, tuning forks, and singing bowls to restore balance and reduce stress. By tuning into various sound frequencies, the human mind and body can experience deep relaxation, improved sleep, and enhanced mood.

    Nearby Sound Therapy Centers in Mumbai

    1. Naad Wellness Studio

    Located in Bandra, Naad Wellness Studio offers therapeutic sessions with Tibetan bowls and gongs, focusing on holistic well-being.

    2. Anahata Sound Healing

    Anahata Sound Healing in Juhu specializes in meditation and chakra healing through sound, using instruments like the monochord and singing bowls.

    3. Inner Space Healing

    Based in Andheri, Inner Space Healing focuses on sound baths and group meditation sessions that promote relaxation and inner peace.
